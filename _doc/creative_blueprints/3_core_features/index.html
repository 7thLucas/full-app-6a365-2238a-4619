For Solo Casual Player

Core Features

Everything that makes the game instantly fun — no setup, no friction.

🧊

Interactive 3D Board

A fully rendered 3×3×3 grid in the browser. Click any cell across three layers to place your mark.

🤖

Minimax AI — 3 Tiers

Easy (random), Medium (depth 2), Hard (depth 4 + alpha-beta pruning). Pick your challenge level.

🏆

Full 3D Win Detection

Rows, columns, pillars, face diagonals, space diagonals — every possible three-in-a-row checked in real time.

⚡

Instant Restart

One tap, new game. The loop stays tight — no menus, no loading, no friction between sessions.

🚫

Zero Sign-Up

Open the page and play immediately. No accounts, no email, no permissions — just the game.

📊

Score Tracking

Win/loss/draw counts persist across games in the session. Scoreboard visible at a glance — toggleable via portal.
